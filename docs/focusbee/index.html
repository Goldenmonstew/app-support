FocusBee

Focus timer & habit tracker for iOS

About

FocusBee helps you stay productive with Pomodoro-style focus sessions, ambient sounds, habit tracking, and detailed statistics. All your data stays on your device — nothing is ever uploaded to a server.

  • Customizable Pomodoro timer with short and long breaks
  • Ambient sound mixer (rain, forest, café, and more)
  • Habit tracking with streaks and daily goals
  • Focus statistics and session history
  • Apple Health integration (Mindful Minutes)
  • Requires iOS 17 or later

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a focus session?

Open FocusBee, set your desired focus duration, and tap the Start button. You can customize session length, break duration, and ambient sounds in Settings.

Does FocusBee work with Apple Health?

Yes. FocusBee can record your focus sessions as Mindful Minutes in Apple Health. You can enable this in Settings > Health Integration.

How do I track my habits?

Go to the Habits tab to create and manage your daily habits. FocusBee tracks your streaks and shows your progress over time.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. Your subscription will be restored automatically if you are signed in with the same Apple ID.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. Go to Settings > Manage Subscription, or open the Settings app on your device > Apple ID > Subscriptions > FocusBee.

Can I use FocusBee without an internet connection?

Yes. All features work offline. Your data is stored locally on your device.
