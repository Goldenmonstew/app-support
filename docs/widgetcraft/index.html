WidgetCraft

Widgets, wallpapers & icon packs for iOS

About

WidgetCraft lets you personalize your home screen with beautiful widgets, wallpapers, and custom app icon packs. All content is processed on your device — no data is ever uploaded to a server.

  • Beautiful home screen widgets
  • Curated wallpaper collections
  • Custom app icon packs
  • 100% on-device processing
  • Requires iOS 17 or later
