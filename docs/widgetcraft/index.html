Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a widget to my home screen?

Open WidgetCraft, customize your widget in the editor, then tap Save. Next, long-press your home screen, tap the + button in the top corner, search for “WidgetCraft”, and add the widget.

How do I apply a custom icon pack?

Go to the Icon Packs tab, select a pack, then follow the on-screen instructions to create Shortcuts for each app icon.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. Your subscription will be restored automatically if you are signed in with the same Apple ID.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. Go to Settings > Manage Subscription, or open the Settings app on your device > Apple ID > Subscriptions > WidgetCraft.

My widget shows a placeholder instead of my customization.

Make sure you have saved your widget in the editor. If the issue persists, try removing the widget from your home screen and adding it again.

The weather widget is not showing data.

The weather widget requires location access. Go to your device Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > WidgetCraft and set it to “While Using the App” or “Always”.

How do I unlock premium features?

Go to the Settings tab and tap “Upgrade to Pro”. You can choose Weekly, Yearly, or Lifetime subscriptions. Premium features include all widget templates, wallpapers, and icon packs.

About

WidgetCraft lets you personalize your home screen with beautiful widgets, wallpapers, and custom app icon packs. All content is processed on your device — no data is ever uploaded to a server.

  • 10 customizable widget types (Clock, Calendar, Countdown, Quote, Weather, Battery, Now Playing, Launcher, Habit, Photo)
  • Curated wallpaper collections
  • Custom app icon packs
  • 100% on-device processing
  • Requires iOS 17 or later
