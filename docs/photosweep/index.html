PhotoSweep

Smart photo cleanup tool for iOS

About

PhotoSweep helps you find and remove duplicate and similar photos from your library, freeing up storage space. All processing happens entirely on your device — your photos never leave your phone.

  • Smart duplicate and similar photo detection
  • Side-by-side photo comparison
  • Batch cleanup with one tap
  • 100% on-device processing
  • Requires iOS 17 or later
